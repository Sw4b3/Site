Welcome

The purpose of this site is to raise awareness of people diagnosed with Asperger’s disorder. The aim is to people with this disorder to get support and as well as educate the general public. There are millions of people who are affected by Autism, both the people who suffer from it and their families. By rasing awareness and giving a platform to both help support people who suffer from this and educated the general public we can improve the quality of life.
